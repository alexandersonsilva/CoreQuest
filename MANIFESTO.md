# CoreQuest Manifesto

## Why CoreQuest Exists

CoreQuest is more than a repository.

It is a long-term engineering journey to understand how computer systems work—from silicon to software.

The goal is not simply to learn how to use technologies, but to understand why they exist and how they work.

Every concept will be studied through theory, experimentation, documentation, and reflection.

---

## Principles

- Learn by understanding, not by memorization.
- Document everything.
- Every question is worth asking.
- Build knowledge layer by layer.
- Curiosity is more important than speed.
- Never copy without understanding.
- Every experiment should teach something.

---

## Mission

To understand computer systems from silicon to software.

---

## Vision

One day, this repository will tell the complete story of a student who started with no experience in Linux, programming, networking, or hardware and gradually developed a deep understanding of computer systems through continuous learning and documentation.
